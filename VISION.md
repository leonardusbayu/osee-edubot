# VISION.md — Product & Business Context

## What this product is trying to become

EduBot should become the most trusted AI-powered English proficiency prep platform for Indonesian learners — covering TOEFL iBT, TOEFL ITP, IELTS, and TOEIC. Not the biggest, not the flashiest. The one that actually gets people real score improvements. From beginner to Band 7+, from first-time test takers to retakers pursuing scholarships, university admission, or career advancement. A student should be able to say: "I started at Band 3.5 and reached Band 6 for my scholarship requirement" — and mean it.

## Who uses this and what they struggle with

Indonesian learners at every level — from false beginners to advanced — preparing for university admission, work abroad, immigration, or professional certification. They can't afford Rp 5-10 million for a preparation course. They have a phone, WhatsApp, Telegram, and 30-60 minutes a day. What they struggle with: not knowing if their pronunciation is correct, feeling lost without a teacher, running out of quality questions, not understanding why they keep getting the same questions wrong, and losing motivation when they can't measure real progress. They'd recommend EduBot if it helped them pass their test — and they'd specifically mention the explanations and feedback that made the difference.

## What success looks like in 12 months

- 3,000 paying premium subscribers across TOEFL iBT, IELTS, TOEFL ITP, and TOEIC tracks
- Average Band/score improvement of 1.0+ among active users who complete 30+ days (tied to real outcomes: scholarship acceptance, job qualification, university admission)
- `@TOEFL_IELTS_Indonesia` channel with 20,000+ engaged followers — recognized as the most valuable English prep content channel in Indonesia
- osee.co.id blog with 50+ articles driving organic search traffic from students searching for "TOEFL tips," "IELTS band 6 how to," etc.
- Speaking evaluation latency under 5 seconds
- Content coverage: minimum 500 questions per test type, all with real explanations
- Zero downtime months

## What you are afraid of

That students practice extensively but their scores don't improve because the explanations are shallow or the AI feedback is too generic — and they leave feeling cheated. That EduBot becomes known as "the cheap option" rather than "the effective one." That the channel content is indistinguishable from every other English prep account — no differentiation, no trust. That a well-funded competitor copies the Telegram Mini App approach and outspends on marketing. That students practice the wrong formats because we haven't matched real test structures closely enough.

## What you refuse to compromise on

- Every question has a real explanation — not just "this is the answer." If we can't write one, we generate one with AI and it must be genuinely helpful.
- We do not inflate confidence scores. If Band 4 is realistic for your current level, we say so — and we show you the exact path to Band 5.
- The free tier is genuinely useful — 10 questions a day that teach something real, regardless of which of the four test types you're preparing for.
- Every feature works on low-end Android with slow internet. No heavy pages, no laggy interactions.
- Content quality on the channel and on osee.co.id sets the standard — not generic tips, but specific, actionable, evidence-based advice that Indonesian students actually can't find elsewhere.
- We serve all four test types (TOEFL iBT, IELTS, TOEFL ITP, TOEIC) with equal care. Not just TOEFL iBT as the flagship.

## Current biggest bottleneck

Content quality is inconsistent across test types and across the channel. IELTS questions have better variety than TOEFL ITP. Some question explanations contradict the answer key. The channel content is text-only — it competes with thousands of similar accounts and doesn't stand out. The explanation enrichment script needs to run for all test types. Students can tell when content was clearly auto-generated vs. curated with care.

## The moat — what makes this hard to copy

- Indonesian learner error patterns captured in spaced repetition data — we know what Indonesian students consistently get wrong at each proficiency level
- The `@TOEFL_IELTS_Indonesia` channel is a built-in audience that's already engaged
- Speaking evaluation trained on Indonesian-accented English — genuinely useful feedback Indonesian learners can't get elsewhere
- osee.co.id blog articles create SEO-driven discovery for students searching for test prep advice
- The referral network creates organic compounding growth (100+ students already invited friends)
- Zero infrastructure cost thanks to Cloudflare free tier — we can price affordably (Rp 99k/month) while maintaining margin
